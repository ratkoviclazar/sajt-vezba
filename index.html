Some of Our Special Services

  • Guided Hiking Tours

    Embark on unforgettable guided hiking tours led by experienced local guides. Explore the hidden gems and stunning vistas of the surrounding mountains.

  • Outdoor Gear Rental

    Forget to pack your hiking gear? No problem! We offer outdoor gear rental services, including hiking poles, backpacks, and rain gear, so you can hit the trails fully equipped.

  • Spa & Wellness Center

    Relax and rejuvenate after a day of hiking at our spa and wellness center. Treat yourself to a massage, unwind in the sauna, or take a refreshing dip in our outdoor pool.

    Enjoy delicious meals crafted from locally sourced ingredients at our on-site restaurant. Fuel up with hearty breakfasts before your hike and unwind with gourmet dinners in the evening.
